Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Description

Cheese can give Ramen Noodles more umph. Especially if you're in College

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 pack of ramen noodles
  • 1 slice of american cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ramen noodles for 2 minutes

  2. Pour out water and add the seasoning packet and cheese

  3. Stir until it is well blended
